Chop and Chips

Pork chops are gifts from heaven, airfry them to get the most crunchy fat ;)

Ingredients

  • Pork chop
  • Bag of frozen fries
  • Frozen peas
  • Salt, pepper and spices

Steps

  1. Spice up pork chops with spices, salt and pepper
  2. Place pork chop in airfryer at medium temperature
  3. Put peas into a plastic container and microwave until cooked
  4. Once chops are done cooking, place in preheated oven at very very low heat
  5. Drop your desired amount of fries into the airfryer until cooked
  6. Plate up all of your items and feed the peasants
