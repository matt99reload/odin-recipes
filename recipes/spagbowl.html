Spagbowl

Spagbowl is very tasty and should be comsumed for breakfast lunch and dinner

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Firstly, chop onions and add them to a hot saucer and cook until almost brown
  2. Now add the mince to your onion and cook till brown
  3. Add sauce to your mince and let come to a boil
  4. Add desired spices and mix well together
  5. Once your mince is simmering away, put your pasta in a pot of boiling water
  6. Add some olive oil and salt to prefered taste to allow pasta to not stick together
  7. Once mince is cooked take of stove and rest for a few minutes
  8. Drain pasta
  9. Serve mince sauce on top of pasta and add some cheese for a great experience
