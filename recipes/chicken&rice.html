Chicken and Rice

A tasty chicken and rice recipe for those lazy Sunday evenings.

Ingredients

  • A whole roast chicken thats cooked (ain't nobody got time to cook a whole chicken on a Sunday night)
  • Rice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Good ol' green beans
  • Corn

Steps

  1. Put one cup of rice into a pot with two cups of water and bring water to simmer
  2. Place corn into boiling water and let cook
  3. Place greenbeans into a plastic container with some butter, salt and pepper, and microwave till cooked
  4. Carve chicken
  5. Place all ingredients onto plate and serve to your hungry family
